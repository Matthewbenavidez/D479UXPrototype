Lodging

Taniti offers lodging to suit every budget and preference. From affordable hostels to a luxurious four-star resort, visitors can choose from a variety of options, including charming family-run hotels and a growing selection of cozy bed and breakfasts. Rest assured, all Tanitian accommodations are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the government, ensuring a comfortable and safe stay.

Four Star Hotels

Taniti's sole four-star resort offers unparalleled luxury with stunning ocean views, world-class dining, and a host of amenities.

Family-run Hotel

Experience authentic Tanitian hospitality at the island's many family-run hotels, where personalized service and local charm create a memorable stay.

Bed and Breakfast

Discover the cozy comfort and home-style breakfasts offered at Taniti's growing number of bed and breakfasts, providing a more intimate and relaxed vacation experience.
