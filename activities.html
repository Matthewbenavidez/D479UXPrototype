Activities

Dive into crystal-clear turquoise waters perfect for snorkeling and discover vibrant coral reefs teeming with marine life. Ride the legendary Tanitian waves with world-class surfing, or embark on a breathtaking boat tour to explore hidden lagoons and volcanic landscapes. Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, Taniti offers an unforgettable island paradise experience.

Snorkeling

Enjoy an underwater wonderland of colorful coral gardens and playful tropical fish.

Surfing

Taniti's surf scene caters to all levels, offering exceptional surf instructors who guide beginners with patience and empower experienced surfers to conquer the island's renowned waves.

Boat Tours

Unveil the island's hidden beauty, from secluded black sand beaches to dramatic volcanic peaks on an unforgettable boat tour.
